Hi! I'm Nicole.

I recently earned my PhD at Northeastern University, in the Center for Cognitive and Brain Health. I'm currently an Assistant Professor at the University of Rhode Island. At the Logan Lab, we research the effects of lifestyle factors, such as physical activity, fitness, and adiposity, mental health, and early life experiences, on cognition and brain structure and function.

The goal of this research is to identify lifestyle interventions which may help to protect against cognitive decline and neural degradation, and moreover enhance executive and brain function, and quality of life.

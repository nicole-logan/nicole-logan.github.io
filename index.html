Hi! I'm Nicole.

I'm a 5th year PhD Candidate at Northeastern University's Center for Cognitive and Brain Health. I research the effects of lifestyle factors, such as physical activity, fitness, and adiposity, on mental health, cognition, and brain structure and function, throughout the lifespan.

The goal of this research is to identify lifestyle interventions which may help to protect against cognitive decline and neural degradation, and moreover enhance executive and brain function, and quality of life.
